Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get asked the most.

Do you ship vehicles nationwide?

Yes, we can arrange shipping anywhere in the continental United States. We work with reputable enclosed and open transport carriers. Contact us for a shipping quote.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Fairfield, California and serve the greater Bay Area for in-person appraisals and consultations. Vehicle sales are available nationwide.

How do vehicle appraisals work?

We come to your location to inspect the vehicle. We provide a detailed written appraisal based on condition, mileage, modifications, and current market value.

Can you help me plan a custom overland build?

Absolutely. We offer build consulting to help you plan everything from suspension and armor to electrical and camping setups. Request a quote to get started.

Do you accept trade-ins?

Yes, we accept trade-ins. Send us your vehicle details through our contact form and we will provide a fair appraisal.
