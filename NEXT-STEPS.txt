=======================================
OVERLAND ROUTE CO. — NEXT STEPS
Post-Launch Checklist
=======================================

Complete these items after the website goes live:

BUSINESS SETUP
--------------
a. Create Google Business Profile for Overland Route Co. in Fairfield, CA
   - Go to: https://business.google.com
   - Add business name, address, phone, hours, photos
   - This is critical for local search visibility

b. Set up social media accounts:
   - Instagram: @overlandrouteco
   - Facebook: facebook.com/overlandrouteco
   - YouTube: youtube.com/@overlandrouteco
   - TikTok: @overlandrouteco
   - Update the social links in the website footer once accounts are live

c. Register for Amazon Associates affiliate program for the Shop page
   - Go to: https://affiliate-program.amazon.com
   - Once approved, replace all "#" links in js/products.js with your affiliate links

d. Apply for BAR/ARD registration at bar.ca.gov
   - Required before offering any repair or maintenance services
   - Once obtained, add registration number to the footer where the HTML comment is

e. Get business license from City of Fairfield
   - Contact: Fairfield City Hall, Business License Division

f. Get seller's permit from CA Board of Equalization (CDTFA)
   - Required for selling vehicles in California
   - Apply at: https://www.cdtfa.ca.gov

g. Consider dealer license from CA DMV if buying/selling vehicles regularly
   - Required if selling more than 5 vehicles per year
   - Apply through CA DMV Occupational Licensing

WEBSITE & ANALYTICS
-------------------
h. Set up Google Analytics
   - Create a GA4 property at https://analytics.google.com
   - Get your Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXXXXX)
   - Add it to js/main.js where it says "YOUR TRACKING ID HERE"

i. Set up Google Search Console and submit sitemap.xml
   - Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Verify ownership of overlandrouteco.com
   - Submit: https://overlandrouteco.com/sitemap.xml

j. Connect email service (Mailchimp) for newsletter signups
   - Create a Mailchimp account at https://mailchimp.com
   - Replace the newsletter form in the footer with Mailchimp's embedded form
   - Newsletter signups are currently stored in browser localStorage as a temporary measure

INSURANCE & LEGAL
-----------------
k. Get business insurance:
   - General liability insurance
   - Garage keeper's insurance (if storing customer vehicles)
   - Commercial auto insurance
   - Errors & omissions (for appraisal services)

l. Set up a business bank account
   - Keep business and personal finances separate
   - Needed for processing vehicle sales and service payments

m. Consider forming an LLC for liability protection
   - File with California Secretary of State
   - Consider consulting with a business attorney
   - An LLC protects personal assets from business liabilities
